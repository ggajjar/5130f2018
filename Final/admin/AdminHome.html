New Option Fall 2018!

Grade Center Sync from Blackboard to SiS: Beginning Fall 2018, faculty will have the option to import final letter grades from your On-Campus Blackboard grade center to your SiS grade roster. Learn more at the On-Campus Blackboard Grade Center synchronization information page.


Grade Center Workshops

Available workshops about On-Campus Blackboard can be found on the Faculty Workshops page. Individual consultations can also be scheduled from the Technology Consultations page.


Known Issue

Safari 11.1 users are experiencing issues uploading files and submitting to assignments. It is recommended to use Chrome or Firefox to avoid the issue.


TA Access

All forms to request access can be found on the Faculty Forms page. The Office of the Registrar grants access to TAs and Graders when the proper contract is confirmed in HR.
